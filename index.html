Gulf Fish Phylogenetics

This website explores the evolutionary relationships among fish species found in Galveston Bay and the Gulf of Mexico, using DNA sequences to ask which species are truly related and which merely look alike.

The site is structured as an undergraduate biology lab: start with why fish relationships matter, learn how DNA builds a tree, explore the trees we built from real GenBank sequences, and then replicate or extend the analysis yourself.
